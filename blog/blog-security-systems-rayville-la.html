Louisiana Licensing: Why It Matters

Important: Louisiana Fire Marshal Licensing

Louisiana law requires all security companies to hold a valid Fire Marshal license. This applies to alarm installation, service, and monitoring. Berkley Security is fully licensed. Before hiring any security company in Rayville, verify their license at the Louisiana State Fire Marshal website.

What happens with an unlicensed installer:

  • Your homeowner's insurance may deny claims related to break-in or fire if the system was installed unlicensed
  • The company can face fines up to $500 per day of unlicensed operation
  • Your monitoring may be invalidated because the monitoring center requires licensed installer credentials
  • You may not qualify for insurance discounts without a licensed installation certificate

Cellular Monitoring for Rural Richland Parish

Many properties in Richland Parish have no broadband internet. This is not a barrier to security. Cellular monitoring communicates through the same cell towers your phone uses:

  • No internet, cable, or phone line required
  • Works anywhere with cell phone reception
  • More secure than internet (cannot be defeated by cutting cables)
  • 24/7 alarm signals sent to the monitoring center via encrypted cellular
