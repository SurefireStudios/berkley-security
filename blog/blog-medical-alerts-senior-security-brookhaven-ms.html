Medical Alert Systems & Senior Security in Brookhaven, MS

Updated April 2026 | By Berkley Security Inc. | 7 min read

Quick Answer:

A medical alert system is a wearable emergency button that connects to a 24/7 monitoring center. When pressed or when a fall is automatically detected, trained operators dispatch EMS to your Brookhaven address within seconds. Systems cost $25 to $45/month with no long-term contract. Berkley Security integrates medical alerts with home security for complete senior protection throughout Lincoln County.

Brookhaven has a significant senior population, and many of those seniors want to remain in their own homes as they age. Aging in place is a wonderful goal, but it comes with real safety concerns: falls, medical emergencies, home security, and the simple fact that adult children often live in Jackson, Hattiesburg, or out of state. A medical alert system bridges that distance, ensuring that help arrives within minutes no matter where family members are.

How Medical Alert Systems Work

Here is what you need to know:

  1. Your parent wears a pendant or wristband at all times, including in the shower (they are waterproof).
  2. In an emergency, they press the button. A powerful speaker in the base station connects them to a trained operator instantly.
  3. The operator assesses the situation through the two-way speaker: "Mrs. Johnson, are you okay? Do you need an ambulance?"
  4. If help is needed, EMS is dispatched immediately to the exact Brookhaven address on file.
  5. Family members are notified by phone that an alert was triggered and what action was taken.

The entire process from button press to EMS dispatch takes under 60 seconds. For a senior who has fallen, is having chest pain, or is experiencing any medical emergency, that speed saves lives.

Fall Detection: Protection When They Cannot Press the Button

The most dangerous scenario is a fall that causes unconsciousness or disorientation. If your parent cannot reach the button, a standard pendant will not help. Automatic fall detection solves this:

  • Built-in accelerometers detect the sudden impact and orientation change of a fall
  • The pendant automatically contacts the monitoring center without any button press
  • The operator speaks through the speaker: "We detected a possible fall. Are you okay?"
  • If there is no response, EMS is dispatched immediately

Fall detection adds approximately $10/month to the base monitoring cost but is the single most important upgrade for seniors living alone.

Frequently Asked Questions: Senior Security in Brookhaven

What is a medical alert system?

A wearable button (pendant or wristband) connected to a 24/7 operator. Press the button and an operator dispatches EMS to your Brookhaven address. Advanced systems detect falls automatically.

How much does a medical alert cost?

$25-$45/month depending on features. Basic pendant: $25/month. With fall detection: $35-$40. Mobile GPS: $35-$45. No long-term contracts required.

What if my parent falls and cannot press the button?

Fall detection pendants automatically contact the monitoring center without a button press. If there is no response from the wearer, EMS is dispatched immediately.

Can medical alerts work with a home alarm?

Yes. We integrate medical alerts with home security. One monitoring center handles intrusion, fire, and medical emergencies. One system, one bill, complete protection.

Does the pendant work outside the home?

In-home pendants work 300-600 ft from the base. Mobile GPS pendants work anywhere with cell service, including driving, shopping, or at church.

Will my family be notified of an alert?

Yes. The monitoring center dispatches EMS and contacts your designated family members by phone. You can list multiple emergency contacts.
