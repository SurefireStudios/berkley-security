National chains advertise free equipment, but you pay for it through inflated monthly rates and long contracts. Here is the math:

  • Monthly rate: $55/month × 60 months = $3,300 total
  • Cancel early: $1,000+ termination fee
  • After contract: Equipment returned, start over

What McComb Homes Need

McComb's mix of older homes, newer subdivisions, and rural properties on the outskirts of town each have different security needs:

  • Older homes downtown: Wireless alarm systems that install without running wires through plaster walls. Door sensors on every exterior entry, motion detectors for common areas.
  • Newer subdivisions: Full smart home integration with smart locks, cameras, automation, and app control.

  • Small businesses on Main Street: Commercial camera systems, access control, and after-hours monitoring.
