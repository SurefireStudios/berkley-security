Intercom Systems for Gated Properties and Multi-Tenant Buildings

Published on March 5, 2026 | By Berkley Security Inc.

Controlling who enters your property is the first line of defense in any security strategy. For gated communities, apartment complexes, and private estates, a professional intercom system provides that critical layer of visitor verification before a gate or door is ever unlocked.

Video Intercom Technology

Modern video intercoms go far beyond the simple buzzer boxes of decades past. Today's systems feature HD cameras, two-way audio, and smartphone integration. When a visitor presses the call button at your gate, you receive a video call on your phone showing exactly who is requesting entry. You can speak with them, verify their identity, and remotely unlock the gate from anywhere in the world.

Multi-Tenant Directory Systems

For apartment buildings and office complexes, directory-style intercoms allow visitors to search for and call specific tenants or businesses. Each unit can independently grant or deny access, and management retains master override capabilities. Entry logs track every access event with timestamps for security auditing.

Gate Integration in Ridgeland

Property managers in Ridgeland, MS rely on Berkley Security for intercom and gate access installations. Our technicians integrate video intercoms with automatic gate operators, ensuring smooth operation and reliable communication even in severe weather. Every system is custom designed to match your property's specific layout and access requirements.
