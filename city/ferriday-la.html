Ferriday Security Questions Answered

Does Berkley serve Ferriday and Concordia Parish?

Yes. We serve all of Concordia Parish: Ferriday, Vidalia, Clayton, Ridgecrest, and Monterey. Our Vicksburg office is just across the river for fast service.

Do I need internet for an alarm system?

No. Every system uses cellular communication. Your alarm connects to our monitoring center over the cell network, not your internet service. Works great in rural Concordia Parish.

What law enforcement gets dispatched?

Depending on your location: Ferriday PD, Vidalia PD, or the Concordia Parish Sheriff's Office. Our monitoring center dispatches the appropriate agency around the clock.

Can you set up cameras for a Highway 84 business?

Yes. We install multi-camera HD systems for retail, restaurants, and service businesses along the 84 corridor. Remote viewing from our free app is included.

Are systems reliable during river parish storms?

Yes. Battery backup keeps the panel running and cellular communication keeps it connected to our center during power outages and severe weather common in Concordia Parish.

What does a Ferriday home security system cost?

Alarm-only systems start at $199. Camera and alarm packages run $500 to $1,500+. Free in-home estimates and financing for Concordia Parish residents.

Is Berkley licensed in Louisiana?

Yes. Fully licensed in Louisiana and Mississippi. Every technician is background-checked and insured. In continuous operation since 1978.
