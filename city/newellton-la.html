Lake Houses Need Security Even When You Are Not There

Many properties around Lake St. Joseph sit empty during the week or for months at a time. That vacancy is a vulnerability. A cellular alarm system monitors doors, windows, and motion sensors around the clock and dispatches the Tensas Parish Sheriff the moment something is wrong. You get a phone call and app notification at the same time.

Cameras give you eyes on the property from anywhere. Pull up a live feed on your phone during lunch to check on the lake house. Review footage if something triggers a motion alert. Our weatherproof outdoor cameras handle Louisiana humidity, rain, and wildlife without issue.

For properties without electricity or internet, we install solar-powered cellular cameras. They charge during the day and record at night, sending alerts directly to your phone. No wiring, no monthly internet bill, and no need to drive out just to check on things.
