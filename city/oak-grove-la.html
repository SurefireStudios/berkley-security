Rural Property Needs a Different Kind of Security

Security companies in cities design systems for houses 20 feet apart with police five minutes away. That is not Oak Grove. Out here, your nearest neighbor might be across a field, your driveway might be a quarter mile long, and the local deputies could take 15 or 20 minutes to reach you.

That reality changes the equation. A monitored alarm system that contacts law enforcement the instant a sensor is triggered saves critical time. Driveway cameras that alert you when someone turns onto your property give you early warning. And none of it requires cable internet, because every system we build uses the cell network.

For agricultural properties, we install solar-powered cellular cameras on equipment barns, field gates, and access roads. They work completely off-grid.
