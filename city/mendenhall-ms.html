Mendenhall's Trusted Security Partner in Simpson County

Mendenhall is the county seat of Simpson County, a small but proud community along the Highway 49 corridor south of Jackson. Surrounded by the piney woods and rolling hills of central Mississippi, Mendenhall is defined by its strong community values, historic courthouse square, and the timber and poultry industries that drive the local economy. The Strong River runs through the area, adding scenic beauty and creating the kind of peaceful, rural setting that Simpson County families treasure.

Whether you need a home security system for your family near the courthouse, surveillance cameras for your Highway 49 business, a commercial alarm for your poultry operation or timber company, or cellular cameras for your hunting camp in the piney woods, our licensed Mississippi technicians design every system for your specific property.
