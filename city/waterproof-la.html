Built for River Country Conditions

Waterproof is a small community on Highway 65 in southern Tensas Parish, sitting close to the Mississippi River levee. The conditions here are unique: high humidity year-round, severe thunderstorms, occasional flooding threats, and power outages that can last for days after major weather events.

Standard internet-dependent security systems fail under exactly these conditions. Our approach is different. Every Berkley alarm panel uses cellular communication that operates on the cell tower network, completely independent of your home internet. Battery backup runs the system for hours after the power goes out. Your security does not stop working when you need it most.

Our Vicksburg headquarters is just across the Mississippi River, making Waterproof one of the closest Louisiana communities we serve. When you need service, we do not have far to travel.
