Protecting New Roads and Pointe Coupee Parish

New Roads is the parish seat of Pointe Coupee Parish, beautifully situated on the shores of False River, an oxbow lake of the Mississippi River. Known for its French Creole heritage, historic homes, and world-class fishing, New Roads is a community where families invest in quality properties that deserve professional security.

False River lakefront homes often feature docks, boathouses, and waterfront access points that need specialized camera coverage. Our HD surveillance systems are designed for waterfront properties with weather-resistant cameras covering docks, shorelines, and outdoor entertaining areas. For rural properties across Pointe Coupee Parish, our cellular monitoring works without broadband.

Every installation includes 24/7 professional monitoring, smartphone control, and a 1-year parts and labor warranty.
