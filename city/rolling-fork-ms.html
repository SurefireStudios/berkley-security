Intrusion Alarm Systems

Surveillance Cameras

Weatherproof HD cameras with infrared night vision. Watch live from your phone, review recorded footage, and get motion alerts. Ideal for farm shops, grain bins, and equipment yards.

Rolling Fork Security Questions

What security options work in a rural Delta town like Rolling Fork?

We install cellular alarm systems that communicate over the cell network instead of cable internet. This is the best option for Rolling Fork and rural Sharkey County properties where broadband is limited.

How fast can you install a system in Rolling Fork?

Most home alarm installations are done in one visit. Camera systems for larger farm properties may take a full day. Our Vicksburg headquarters is about 45 minutes away.

Can the cameras handle Delta heat and storms?

Yes. All outdoor cameras are IP66 weather-rated for extreme heat, humidity, rain, and wind. Battery backup keeps everything running during power outages.

Who responds when an alarm goes off?

Our 24/7 monitoring center contacts Rolling Fork Police or the Sharkey County Sheriff immediately. You are also notified via phone and app.

What does a system cost for a Rolling Fork home?

Basic alarm packages start around $199. Camera systems run $500 to $1,500+ depending on the number of cameras and property size. Free on-site estimates available.

Can I watch my farm cameras from my phone?

Yes. Every camera system comes with a free app. Pull up live views, playback recordings, and receive motion alerts from anywhere with cell service.

Is Berkley licensed and insured in Mississippi?

Absolutely. Mississippi-licensed since 1978, also licensed in Louisiana. Every technician passes a background check and carries liability insurance.
