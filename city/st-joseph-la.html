The Parish Seat Across the River From Our Front Door

St. Joseph is directly across the Mississippi River from our Vicksburg, Mississippi headquarters. That geographic proximity gives us an advantage that no other security company can match in Tensas Parish. When you need service, we are right across the bridge.

As the parish seat, St. Joseph houses the courthouse, local government offices, and the businesses that support the community. Our cellular alarm systems work everywhere the cell network reaches, with battery backup that keeps the system running during the power outages that river storms bring.
