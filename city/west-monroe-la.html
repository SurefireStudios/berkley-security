A Growing City Needs Smart Security

West Monroe and Monroe together make up the largest metro in northeast Louisiana. The twin cities on the Ouachita River are home to shopping centers, hospitals, universities, and a steady stream of new residential construction. Thomas Road, Trenton Street, and the I-20 interchange areas have seen rapid commercial development in recent years.

More people and more businesses mean more property to protect. A professionally monitored alarm system provides the response time advantage that standalone cameras and doorbell apps simply cannot. When an alarm is triggered, our center immediately contacts West Monroe PD or the Ouachita Parish Sheriff to dispatch officers. No delays and no voicemail.

Our Vicksburg headquarters connects directly to West Monroe via I-20. Our technicians travel the corridor regularly and know the area well. Whether it is a new build in a Sterlington subdivision or a camera upgrade for a restaurant on Trenton Street, we deliver the same custom, professional service we have been known for since 1978.

Retail & Office Cameras

West Monroe Residents Ask

Do you cover the whole Monroe-West Monroe metro?

Yes. All of Ouachita Parish: West Monroe, Monroe, Sterlington, Brownsville, Calhoun, and surrounding areas.

Can you install cameras for a Thomas Road business?

Yes. We design commercial surveillance for retail, restaurants, and offices throughout West Monroe's commercial corridors including Thomas Road, I-20, and Trenton Street.

What happens during a Louisiana storm?

Battery backup and cellular monitoring keep your system operational. Even during extended power outages and internet failures, your alarm stays connected to our 24/7 center.

Why not just go with a national chain?

We are regionally based with local technicians. You get custom system design, direct communication, and no outsourced support. In business since 1978 with real accountability.

Do you work with new subdivision builders?

Yes. We coordinate with builders during framing to pre-wire alarm and camera systems. Cleaner installation, lower cost, and the system is ready when you move in.
