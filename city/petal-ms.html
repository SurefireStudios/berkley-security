New Build? Wire It Right From the Start.

Petal is one of the fastest-growing cities in southern Mississippi. New subdivisions are going up along the Leaf River corridor and east of town near the I-59 interchange. If you are building, now is the time to include security wiring in your plans.

Pre-wiring during the framing stage means alarm sensors and camera cables run cleanly inside the walls. No exposed wires. No surface-mount channels. The system looks like it was always part of the house because it was. And it costs less than retrofitting after the drywall is up.

Talk to your builder and have them call us. We coordinate directly with the construction crew to get the wiring placed before inspection. When you move in, the system is ready to activate.

Already in an existing home? No problem. We install wireless and hybrid systems that look clean and work reliably without tearing into your walls.

Questions From Petal Homeowners

Can you pre-wire a new build in Petal for security?

Yes. We coordinate with builders during framing to run alarm and camera wiring inside the walls. Call us before drywall goes up for the best results and lowest cost.

How is professional monitoring better than a DIY system?

DIY sends a phone alert. Professional monitoring connects to a live operator who verifies the alarm and dispatches Petal Police or Forrest County Sheriff immediately. That human response matters in a real emergency.

Do you work with Highway 42 businesses?

Yes. We install multi-camera surveillance, intrusion alarms, and fire systems for retail, restaurants, and offices along the Highway 42 corridor.

Will my pets set off the motion sensors?

No. We set up pet-immune sensors that ignore animals under a certain weight. Your dog and cat move freely without triggering false alarms.

Why choose Berkley over a national company?

We have been Mississippi-based since 1978. No outsourced call centers, no door-to-door salespeople, no long-term contract traps. Local technicians, local decisions.

What does a Petal system cost?

Basic alarms start at $199. Camera systems run $500 to $1,500+. New construction pre-wiring is significantly cheaper. Free estimates and financing available.

Do you serve Oak Grove and Lamar County too?

Yes. We cover the entire Hattiesburg metro: Petal, Oak Grove, Lamar County, and all of Forrest County.
