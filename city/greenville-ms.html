Why Greenville Businesses and Homeowners Trust Berkley

Greenville is a city of contrasts. The downtown revival brings new restaurants and shops, while the Highway 82 commercial strip stretches for miles with big-box retail, car lots, and convenience stores. Meanwhile, quieter neighborhoods off Washington Avenue and Percy Street reflect generations of families who have built their lives here along the Mississippi River.

Each of those environments has different security needs. A downtown boutique needs discreet interior cameras and a point-of-sale alarm. A catfish processing plant on the edge of town needs perimeter cameras with license-plate recognition and after-hours motion alerts. A family home near the levee needs entry sensors, glass-break detectors, and a system that stays operational during storm season power outages.

We have been solving these problems since 1978. Our headquarters in Vicksburg is an easy drive east on Highway 82, which means our technicians can reach Greenville quickly for installations, service, and emergency calls.

Greenville Security: Common Questions

Which security company has the most experience in the Greenville area?

Berkley Security has operated continuously since 1978. We are one of the longest-running security providers in the region, licensed in both Mississippi and Louisiana.

Do you handle Highway 82 commercial properties?

Yes. We install multi-camera surveillance, intrusion alarms, and fire systems for retail, office, and restaurant properties along the Highway 82 corridor and throughout downtown.

What happens to my alarm during a Delta thunderstorm?

Battery backup keeps the panel running and cellular communication keeps it connected to our monitoring center. Your system stays armed even when the power grid goes down.

Can you secure a catfish plant or processing facility?

Absolutely. We design commercial-grade camera systems with perimeter detection, access control, and monitored fire alarms for industrial and food processing properties.

What parts of Washington County do you cover?

All of Washington County including Leland, Hollandale, Winterville, Metcalfe, and the surrounding agricultural areas. Cellular monitoring works for rural properties outside city limits.

How much should I budget for a camera system?

A 4-camera home package starts around $500. Commercial systems with more cameras and advanced recording range from $1,000 to $3,000+. We offer free estimates and financing.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We offer flexible monthly financing so homeowners and business owners can get the system they need without a large upfront payment.
