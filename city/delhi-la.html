Professional Security for Every Richland Parish Property

Delhi sits along Highway 80 between Tallulah and Rayville in the heart of Richland Parish. The town is surrounded by farmland, timber, and hunting leases that stretch for miles in every direction. What We Install in Delhi & Richland Parish

Delhi & Richland Parish FAQ

Can you really serve a small town like Delhi?

Absolutely. We serve communities of every size. Delhi and Richland Parish are on our regular service route via I-20. Cellular monitoring works here just as well as in any city.

Do alarms work without internet out here?

Yes. Cellular is our standard. The alarm connects to our center over the cell network. No cable modem, no DSL, no phone line needed.

Which agency responds to a Delhi alarm?

Delhi PD in town, Richland Parish Sheriff outside city limits. Our monitoring center dispatches the right agency automatically, 24/7.

Can cameras work on a hunting lease?

Yes. Cellular cameras work without internet. Solar-powered options available for sites with no electricity. Motion alerts sent directly to your phone.

How far away is your office?

About an hour east on I-20 from Vicksburg. Our technicians travel through Richland Parish regularly for installations and service.

What does a Delhi home system cost?

Alarms from $199. Camera packages $500 to $1,500+. Free on-site estimates and financing for Richland Parish residents.

Are you licensed to work in Louisiana?

Yes. Dual-licensed in Louisiana and Mississippi since 1978. Insured, background-checked technicians.
