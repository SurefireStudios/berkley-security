Frequently Asked Questions

Can you install cameras at a cabin on Grenada Lake?

Yes. Cellular cameras are ideal for seasonal and lakefront properties. They operate over the cell network with no cable internet needed. Monitor your cabin from anywhere on your phone.

What makes you different from a national alarm company?

We have been locally based in Mississippi since 1978. Our technicians know the area. We build custom solutions rather than forcing you into a one-size-fits-all national package.

Do you serve I-55 businesses in Grenada?

Yes. We install multi-camera surveillance, intrusion alarms, and fire systems for hotels, restaurants, gas stations, and retail along the interstate corridor.

Why does cellular monitoring matter in Grenada County?

Cell-based systems do not depend on cable internet or landlines. They stay connected during power outages and storms, which matters in rural areas with limited broadband coverage.

What is the price range for a Grenada home system?

Basic alarm systems start at $199. Camera installations range from $500 to $1,500+. We visit your property for a free assessment to give you an exact number.

Do cameras hold up through Mississippi summers?

Yes. We use commercial-grade cameras rated for extreme heat and humidity. Night vision provides clear footage after dark. They are built to last in Mississippi conditions.

Do you serve outside Grenada city limits?

Yes. We cover all of Grenada County, including Gore Springs, Holcomb, Duck Hill, and the Grenada Lake area.
