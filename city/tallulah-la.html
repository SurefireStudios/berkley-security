Closest to You, Fastest to Respond

Madison Parish sits on the Louisiana side of the Mississippi River, right along the I-20 corridor between Vicksburg and Monroe. Our Vicksburg headquarters is about 30 minutes east on the interstate, making Tallulah one of the closest Louisiana communities we serve.

Security Solutions for Madison Parish

Residential Alarm Packages

I-20 Commercial Cameras

Tallulah & Madison Parish: Your Questions

Does Berkley serve Tallulah and all of Madison Parish?

Yes. All of Madison Parish including Tallulah, Delta, Mound, and Richmond. Our Vicksburg headquarters is about 30 minutes east on I-20.

Can you handle an I-20 gas station or truck stop?

Yes. We design multi-camera systems with license plate capture, night vision, and remote viewing for high-traffic I-20 commercial properties.

Does the system stay on during power outages?

Yes. Battery backup and cellular communication keep your system armed and connected to our monitoring center even during extended outages and severe weather.

Who responds to a Tallulah alarm?

Tallulah Police for in-town properties. Madison Parish Sheriff for unincorporated areas. Dispatched automatically by our 24/7 center.

Can cameras work on a Madison Parish farm?

Yes. Cellular cameras operate without broadband. View equipment areas and access points from your phone. Motion alerts sent automatically.

How much does a Tallulah security system cost?

Home alarms start at $199. Camera packages $500 to $1,500+. Commercial installations vary by scope. Free estimates and financing available.

Are you licensed in Louisiana?

Yes. Dual-licensed in Louisiana and Mississippi. Background-checked technicians. In business since 1978.
