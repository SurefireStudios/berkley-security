Key Features

Our fire alarm systems provide comprehensive fire detection and alerting to keep your property and people safe.

Smoke Detection

Sensitive photoelectric and ionization sensors detect smoke particles early for faster response.

Heat Sensors

Fixed temperature and rate-of-rise detectors for areas where smoke detection isn't suitable.

Pull Stations

Manual fire alarm activation points for building occupants to report fires immediately.

Audible Alarms

Loud horns and strobes ensure everyone in the building is alerted during an emergency.

Code Compliance

Fire codes can be complex and vary by jurisdiction. Our team ensures your system meets all applicable NFPA standards and local fire codes.

NFPA 72 Compliance

All systems designed and installed to National Fire Alarm and Signaling Code standards.

Local AHJ Coordination

We work with Authority Having Jurisdiction officials to ensure smooth permitting and approvals.

Documentation & As-Built Drawings

Complete documentation package for insurance and compliance records.

Inspection & Testing

Regular inspection and testing keeps your fire system reliable and compliant with fire codes.

  • Annual inspection by certified technicians
  • Monthly visual inspections recommended
  • Functional testing of all devices annually
  • 24/7 monitoring with fire department dispatch
  • Remote annunciator monitoring available
