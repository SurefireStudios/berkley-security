Residential vs. Commercial

While both residential and commercial systems share core monitoring technology, each has distinct requirements.

Residential Systems

Focus on ease of use, family safety, and smart home integration. Typically include perimeter detection, interior motion, and mobile app control.

Commercial Systems

Often require fire monitoring integration, multiple user codes, schedule-based arming, and may interface with existing business systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about security alarm systems in Mississippi and Louisiana.

How much does a security alarm system cost in Mississippi?
A professionally installed security alarm system in Mississippi typically costs between $199 and $599 for equipment and installation, plus $25 to $50 per month for 24/7 monitoring. Berkley Security offers free consultations and customized quotes based on your property size and security needs. Many homeowners also qualify for insurance discounts of 5-20% that can offset monitoring costs.
What does 24/7 alarm monitoring include?
24/7 alarm monitoring means a UL-listed central station watches your security system around the clock, 365 days a year. When an alarm triggers, trained operators verify the signal, contact you, and dispatch police, fire, or EMS as needed. Berkley Security uses cellular monitoring for reliable communication even during internet or power outages, which is especially important during Mississippi storm season.
Do I need a permit for a security alarm in Mississippi?
Some Mississippi municipalities require alarm permit registration. Berkley Security handles the permit process for you during installation. False alarm fines vary by city, typically starting at $50 for the third false alarm in a 12-month period. Our professional installation and modern sensors significantly reduce false alarm rates compared to DIY systems.
What is the best security system for a home in Mississippi?
The best home security system in Mississippi includes door and window sensors, motion detectors, a cellular communicator for reliable monitoring, and a touchscreen keypad. For Mississippi homes, cellular monitoring is recommended over internet-only systems due to the frequency of severe weather and power outages in the region. Adding video surveillance and smart home integration provides the most complete protection.
Can I get a security system installed in a rental property?
Yes, Berkley Security installs wireless security systems in rental properties with minimal wall penetration. Wireless systems can be removed when you move. We recommend getting landlord approval before installation, and many landlords welcome security upgrades because they protect their property investment.
