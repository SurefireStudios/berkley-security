Medical Alert Systems

Emergency response at the press of a button. Wearable pendants with 24/7 monitoring give seniors and their families peace of mind.

Call Now Free Consultation

Key Features

Our medical alert systems provide reliable emergency response with features designed for seniors and their caregivers.

Wearable Devices

Lightweight pendant or wristband that can be worn at all times, even in the shower.

24/7 Monitoring

Our monitoring center is staffed around the clock, every day of the year.

One-Touch Response

Simply press the button to speak with a trained emergency operator immediately.

Fall Detection

Optional automatic fall detection can sense when you fall and call for help even if you cannot press the button.

Who It's For

Medical alert systems are ideal for seniors and individuals who want to maintain independence while having emergency support available.

Seniors Living Alone

Stay safe at home with instant access to emergency help at any time.

Individuals with Medical Conditions

Heart disease, diabetes, epilepsy, and other conditions can be monitored.

People at Risk of Falling

Those with mobility issues or osteoporosis benefit from automatic fall detection.

Caregivers and Family Members

Peace of mind for loved ones who cannot be there 24/7.

How It Works

Our medical alert system is simple to use and provides reliable protection.

  1. 1 Wear your pendant or wristband throughout the day and night.
  2. 2 Press the button whenever you need help - whether it is a fall, medical emergency, or any situation.
  3. 3 Our trained operator will speak with you through the base unit or your wearable device.
  4. 4 We will dispatch emergency services or contact family members based on your needs.
