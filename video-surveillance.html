Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about security camera systems in Mississippi and Louisiana.

How many security cameras do I need for my home?
Most homes in Mississippi need 4 to 8 security cameras for comprehensive coverage. Key locations include front and back doors, the driveway, garage, side yards, and any ground-floor windows not visible from the street. Berkley Security provides a free site assessment to determine the optimal number and placement for your property.
What is the difference between NVR and cloud camera storage?
NVR (Network Video Recorder) stores footage locally on a hard drive at your property with no monthly fees, while cloud storage uploads footage to remote servers for a monthly subscription. Many Berkley Security customers choose hybrid systems that record locally and back up critical clips to the cloud for the best of both worlds.
Can I view my security cameras remotely from my phone?
Yes, all modern security camera systems installed by Berkley Security include remote viewing via smartphone apps. You can watch live feeds, review recorded footage, receive motion alerts, and share clips with law enforcement from anywhere with an internet connection.
Do security cameras work at night?
Yes, professional-grade security cameras include infrared night vision that captures clear footage in complete darkness up to 100+ feet. Berkley Security installs cameras with both infrared and color night vision options, so you get usable footage 24 hours a day regardless of lighting conditions.
How much does a security camera system cost in Mississippi?
A professionally installed security camera system in Mississippi typically costs between $500 and $2,500 depending on the number of cameras, resolution (HD vs 4K), and storage type. Berkley Security offers free consultations and competitive pricing with no hidden fees. Most systems pay for themselves through insurance discounts and theft prevention.
