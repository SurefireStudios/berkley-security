Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about smart home automation in Mississippi and Louisiana.

What can a smart home automation system control?
A smart home automation system can control door locks, lights, thermostats, garage doors, and security cameras from a single app. Berkley Security integrates automation with your alarm system so everything works together, including automated scenes that lock doors, arm the alarm, and adjust the thermostat when you leave.
Do smart locks work with my existing deadbolt?
Most smart locks replace your existing deadbolt and fit standard door preparations. Berkley Security installs Z-Wave smart locks that integrate with your security panel, allowing remote lock/unlock, temporary guest codes, and notifications when doors are locked or unlocked.
Can home automation save money on energy bills?
Yes, smart thermostats and automated lighting can reduce energy costs by 10-25%. In Mississippi's hot summers, smart thermostat savings alone can pay for the automation system within 1-2 years.
Does home automation work during a power outage?
Your security system's battery backup keeps the alarm panel and Z-Wave hub running during power outages. Smart locks continue to work on their own batteries. Cellular monitoring remains active throughout the outage.
How much does home automation cost?
Basic automation with a smart lock and thermostat costs $300-$800. Full-home automation ranges from $1,000-$3,000. Berkley Security offers free consultations to design a system that fits your budget.
